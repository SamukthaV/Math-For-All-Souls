How to add more PDFs

  1. Put your PDF files inside the pdfs/ folder.
  2. Edit index.html and duplicate one of the list items under Research PDFs with your file name.
  3. If you want an inline preview, change the <iframe src="..."> to your new file's path (e.g., pdfs/my-paper.pdf).
